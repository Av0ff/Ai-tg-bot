Here is a short FAQ draft for a stationery store.
Question - What are your delivery options?
Answer: We ship standard (2-5 business days) and express (1-2 business days).

Question - Can I get an invoice for my company?
Answer: Yes, provide your company details and we will issue an invoice.

Question - Do you offer bulk discounts?
Answer: We offer bulk pricing for large orders. Tell us the product and quantity.

Question - What payment methods are accepted?
Answer: We accept card payments and bank transfers.

Question - Can I track my order?
Answer: Yes, send your order number and we will check the status.

Question - What is your return policy?
Answer: Returns are accepted within 14 days if items are unused and in original packaging.

Question - Do you ship internationally?
Answer: Currently we ship only within the country.

Question - Can I cancel or change my order?
Answer: Contact support within 2 hours after purchase to request changes.

Question - Do you sell recycled paper products?
Answer: Yes, we have recycled notebooks and printer paper in stock.

Question - What are your business hours?
Answer: Support is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM.

Question - Do you provide samples for corporate clients?
Answer: Samples are available for bulk orders on request.

Question - How do I apply a discount code?
Answer: Enter the code at checkout before completing payment.

Question - Can you bundle items into a single shipment?
Answer: Yes, we consolidate items when possible to reduce shipping costs.

Question - Do you offer gift wrapping?
Answer: Gift wrapping is available for an additional fee.

Question - Are there minimum order amounts for wholesale?
Answer: Yes, wholesale orders require a minimum quantity per item.

Question - How do I request a refund?
Answer: Contact support with your order number and reason for return.

Extra notes: Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM.
